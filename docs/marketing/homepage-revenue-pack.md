## Objections Handling

### "I already use ChatGPT."
Use it for general work. Use MindReply when the message has trust, money, timing, or follow-up risk and you need a prepared next move plus a receipt.

### "I do not want to share sensitive text."
Remove private details before pasting. The product is designed around consent-gated memory and receipt-first handling. Do not paste secrets, credentials, payment data, or regulated private information.

### "Why pay after the free read?"
Do not pay if the first output does not save time. Pay when the output proves the problem is repeated or the website/follow-up path needs a focused completion pass.

### "Is this a subscription?"
The first paid path is the Website Completion Package. Growth and Pro only make sense when repeat overload, premium review, or deeper continuity is needed.

### "Can I get an invoice?"
Yes. Use the invoice request path with company name, billing email, country, and project outcome.
