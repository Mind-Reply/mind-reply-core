# Owner Intent Dictionary

When the owner uses shorthand or ambitious language, translate it into safe, evidence-backed execution tasks. Do not take phrases literally when they could lead to unsafe, unethical, or platform-violating actions.

## Intent Mappings

### "make it viral"

**Does NOT mean:** Buy followers, spam platforms, create fake engagement, or use bots.

**Means:** Create compliant launch hooks, a distribution plan with tracking, and owner-approved publishing. Prepare shareable assets, SEO, OG tags, and a measured outreach plan. Nothing goes live without owner approval.

---

### "human-like"

**Does NOT mean:** Create fake personas, impersonate real people, or use deceptive AI-generated profiles.

**Means:** Founder-led, brand-owned, transparent human layer. All public-facing content is clearly attributed to the real owner or brand. No fake bios, no synthetic testimonials, no manufactured social proof.

---

### "stealth"

**Does NOT mean:** Hide the product, deceive users, or obscure business identity.

**Means:** Premium understated positioning. No loud launch announcements. Clean, confident presence. Let the product speak. Invite-only or controlled access if appropriate.

---

### "fully automatic"

**Does NOT mean:** Deploy, post, send, or bill without any human oversight.

**Means:** Automate safe workflows (builds, monitoring, reports, drafts). Approval required before: sending messages, posting to social, deploying to production, billing customers, or any action that cannot be undone.

---

### "make revenue today"

**Does NOT mean:** Charge random people, create fake invoices, or make unverified payment claims.

**Means:** Fastest legitimate revenue path. Priority order:
1. Live offer page with clear CTA
2. Working contact/request route
3. Invoice-first option for warm leads
4. Owner-approved outreach drafts
5. Stripe checkout verified in test mode before going live

---

### "20-30 sites/day"

**Does NOT mean:** Copy-paste empty pages, spin up domains with no content, or count broken deploys.

**Means:** Controlled site factory only after 2 verified sites pass full smoke tests (URL works, HTTPS works, CTA works, monitoring exists, evidence logged). Scale only after the factory automation is proven. Each site must have: unique brand, clear offer, working contact, privacy page, copyright notice, and monitoring.

---

### "secure this chat"

**Does NOT mean:** Encrypt the conversation or hide it from the platform.

**Means:** Save owner context, operating rules, and session state into private repo files (this file and its companions). No secret values stored. Session checkpoints enable continuity across resets.

---

### "make it premium"

**Does NOT mean:** Add expensive features or over-engineer.

**Means:** Dark, sharp, non-generic visual identity. Clean typography. Purposeful whitespace. No stock photo energy. No commodity SaaS aesthetics. Every surface should feel intentional and built for control.

---

### "ship it"

**Does NOT mean:** Push to production without checks.

**Means:** Open PR, pass build, run smoke test, deploy to preview first. Production deploy only after validation and owner approval for anything customer-facing.

---

### "connect everything"

**Does NOT mean:** Grant all tools full access to everything.

**Means:** Map integrations (GitHub, Vercel, n8n, Stripe, Zapier) into one ops engine with least-privilege access. Each connector has a defined scope and documented purpose. Owner cockpit shows status of all connections.
